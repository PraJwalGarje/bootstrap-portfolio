Note From Zero

  • We will call them the [numbered doors]. The doors in front of you are a pair of the same.

  • The key to opening these numbered doors are the [numbered bracelets] that each of you possess.

  • Should you total the numbers on your [numbered bracelets] and find that the [digital root] of that number is equal to the number of that door, the door will open.

  • Only those who have opened the door may pass through.

  • There are, however, limits.

  • Only [3-5] people can pass through one numbered door. All those who enter must leave, and all who enter must contribute.

  • The purpose of the game is simple. Leave this ship alive.

  • It is hidden, but an exit can be found. Seek a way out...Seek a door that carries a [9].

Survivor's Guide

RED

Any bracelets that pass through a numbered door will start an 81 second countdown, after which they will detonate the bomb that has been ingested by its wearer.

An opened numbered door will automatically close after 9 seconds.

DEAD

To deactivate, all bracelets that were verified by the RED outside the door must be re-verified by the DEAD found somewhere past the door.

The DEAD will not activate until after the numbered door has closed.

Conditions

Bracelets can only be removed once A) they pass through the ship's exit or B) their wearer's heart rate reaches 0.

Attempts to remove the bracelet without meeting either requirement will cause the connected bomb to detonate.
